Welcome to ShareTaxi

ShareTaxi allows you to easily plan a taxi route with multiple stops, for sharing a cab with friends.

Plan a Route

Starting or ending at different places? Just key them in; we will optimise the route for you!

Share a Route

Not with a friend right now? Try opening a route up for sharing. Just make sure you give enough time for your friends to reply!

Find a Route

Your friends may also be sharing routes. You can find these from the side menu, and send requests to join their routes there.

That's it!
